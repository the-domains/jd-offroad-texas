MegaRun 2016

We had a fantastic time out at MegaRun this year. As usual it is time to test out all of the upgrades that we've installed during our Cold (um cough, not any where near cold) Winter months. This year we took on a project truck, Code Named: Ice Pack. We had 6 months and went decided to see how far we could change it up to and see if we could make it into a real off road machine. We started with a 1992 Dodge W250 long bed work truck. We started with the essentials: Winch, Tires, Bumper, Rock Rails, Front and Rear Lockers, 6" Lift, and Pulled the bed and shortened the frame for length. We believe we may have really exceeded what we aimed for. IcePack worked perfect out on the trails. We aimed it at a hill or mud puddle and it powered right on through.
